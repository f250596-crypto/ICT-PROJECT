Admissions Information

Learn about the admission requirements and process of FAST CFD Campus

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Intermediate / A-Levels
  • Minimum 60% marks or equivalent
  • Must appear in FAST Admission Test
  • Original documents required for verification

Admission Process

  1. Create an account on FAST admission portal
  2. Fill out the online application form
  3. Select your degree program
  4. Appear in the FAST Entry Test
  5. Wait for the merit list
  6. Submit documents and pay admission fee
  7. Collect ID card and join classes

Required Documents

  • CNIC or B-Form
  • Matric Certificate
  • Intermediate Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Entry Test Result Slip
