Our Services

  • Campus Tours: Guided tours for new students and visitors to explore the campus.
  • Admissions Counseling: Help and advice regarding admission procedures and requirements.
  • Student Support: Assistance with academic, personal, and career-related issues.
  • Library Access: Resources and study spaces for students and faculty.
  • IT Support: Helpdesk for technical support and campus network issues.
  • Events & Workshops: Regular activities to promote learning and networking.

Alumni Network & Career Support

FAST University takes pride in its strong alumni network spread across top companies worldwide. The campus offers career counseling, CV-building workshops, mock interviews, and alumni meetups to help students build connections and shape successful careers.

Career Counseling

One-on-one sessions to guide students in career decision-making.

Industry Meetups

Events connecting students with alumni working in tech and engineering industries.

Internship Support

Assistance in finding internships through alumni referrals and job portals.

Health & Sports Facilities

To promote physical well-being along with academics, the campus provides multiple sports facilities and a basic medical care unit for students.

  • Sports Grounds: Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Basketball, Futsal, and Badminton.
  • Gym Area: Indoor fitness space for students.
  • Medical Room: Basic first-aid and health support available on campus.
