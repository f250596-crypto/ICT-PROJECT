About FAST CFD Campus

FAST-NU Chiniot–Faisalabad (CFD) Campus is one of the modern and fastest-growing campuses of the National University of Computer & Emerging Sciences. Known for its peaceful environment and quality education, it provides students with advanced labs, experienced faculty, and a strong learning culture. The campus focuses on technology, innovation, and professionalism, helping students build both academic and practical skills.
